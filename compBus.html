Who We are

We are a non-profit organization committed to take technology to under-served youth in villages of India. We visit villages to teach the benefits and usage of Internet.

Our Mission:
  • Inspire to break the barriers of poverty and cast system restrictions via exposure to technology
  • Create entrepreneurs to solve local problems
  • Open world of internet possibilities

How can you contribute

We need your help! You can contribute in so many ways:

  • Spread the Word on Social Media
  • Donate used computers, mobile devices, or any tech toys
  • Join our 7 week volunteer program in India or you can join us here in Provo
  • Have your organization donate or sponsor

Curriculum

Some of the featured classroom curriculum will include:

  • Impact of Internet and Social Media
  • Internet Entrepreneurship
  • Introduction to Computer Software Programming
  • Leveraging and Impacting local community
