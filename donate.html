Our Approximate Launch Costs

This good cause comes with some costs

Our Expense:
  • Purchase Used Bus, Modify the bus to fit our needs: ~ $4000 (one time cost)
  • Mobile Internet: ~ $30 per month
  • Advertisements: ~$200 semi annually
  • Equipment (computers and printers): ~$3000 (one time cost)

Self-Sustaining Plan
