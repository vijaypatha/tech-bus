Who We are

Internet Cafe Bus, an education non-profit organization, takes digital literacy to under served communites in fomer Andhra Pradesh, India. We introduce internet, it's usage, and benefits, and bring them "online".

Our Mission:
  • Inspire to break the barriers of economical and social poverty
  • Create entrepreneurs to solve local problems
  • Provide tools to be successful in this internet age

How you can contribute

We need your help! You can contribute in so many ways:

  • Hop on "the bus" to make this a reality
  • Have your organization donate or sponsor
  • Spread the Word on Social Media
  • Donate used computers or laptops

Curriculum
